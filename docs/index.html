Our Services

introduction

In 2003, the first "BeFit Fitness Studio" opened its doors in Ostkreuz under the name "Get Fit First".

The small, family-run gym came to life in a former tennis hall. For only 10 EURO per month, it was affordable for young and old and quickly developed into a popular meeting place. Expansion occurred and the popular family atmosphere of the "Get Fit First" gyms reached other districts in Berlin and Brandenburg.

Over time, the previously exclusive fitness offering was expanded to include saunas, showers and trainer support, as well as wi-fi and a variety of courses. We were one of the first fitness centres to offer a flat rate for drinks.

In the studios, customers always called us "BeFit" for short, so it became "BeFit". To this day, the family-run "BeFit Fitnessclub" stands for a family atmosphere, good value for money and personal care. We offer a variety of people a meeting place and not just an anonymous fitness hall. We look forward to being or becoming this meeting place for you in the future.
